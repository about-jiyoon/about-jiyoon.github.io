Korea Sommelier of the Year 2019

Introduction

Korea Sommelier of the Year held for the fifth time with the great improvement, valuable expansion and rising reputation. The competition was founded to advance Korean wine culture and foster awareness among the Korean public about wine and sommeliers. It is the only competition in Korea that is not limited to one country or region but encompasses the whole world and all of its important wine producing countries and regions.
