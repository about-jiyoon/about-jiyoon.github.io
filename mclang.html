MCLANG SALOON

Introduction

Mclang Saloon introduces 6 different beers a week and archives stories of brew masters, craft beers and the breweries. It also produces a “Drink Map” yearly that catalogues all the local breweries in Korea, their locations and their contact information. All small breweries that are supported by Mclang have a bottle label with the semi-circular Mclang graphic. Mclang Saloon seeks to be the information hub for Korean craft beer and to provide constantly up-to-date craft beer guides for beginners, as well as other craft-beer-related merchandise for the community.
